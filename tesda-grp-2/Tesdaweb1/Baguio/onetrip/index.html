Mines View Park

Mines View Park in Baguio, Philippines, is a popular tourist
destination known for its scenic views of abandoned mining
sites and the surrounding mountains. Visitors can shop for
souvenirs and locally made crafts, including traditional Cordillera clothing and handicrafts. The park offers a glimpse into the
region's mining history and provides an opportunity to appreciate
the natural beauty of the area..

Baguio Botanical Garden

The Botanical Garden in Baguio City is a serene oasis nestled within the bustling urban landscape. It features a rich collection of indigenous and exotic plant species, showcasing the
natural beauty of the region. Visitors can explore lush greenery, serene pathways, and traditional Igorot huts, making it a perfect destination for nature enthusiasts and those seeking a peaceful retreat in the heart of the city.

Burnham Park

Burnham Park, located in Baguio City, Philippines, is a serene urban park offering a wide range of recreational activities. It features a picturesque man-made lake where visitors can rent rowboats, as well as gardens, walking paths, and a skating rink. The park is a popular spot for picnics, leisurely strolls, and enjoying the cool climate of Baguio.

Our Lady of Atonement Cathedral

Our Lady of the Atonement Cathedral, also known as Baguio
Cathedral, is a stunning Catholic church located in Baguio City, Philippines. The cathedral boasts distinctive architecture with
twin spires and intricate stained glass windows. It serves as a significant religious and cultural landmark in the city, drawing
visitors and devotees alike to its serene ambiance and
panoramic views of Baguio.

Camp Jhon Hay

Camp John Hay, located in Baguio City, Philippines, is a historic
former rest and recreation facility for the United States Armed
Forces. Today, it has been transformed into a popular resort and recreational area, featuring a golf course, hotels, restaurants, and scenic hiking trails. Visitors can enjoy the cool climate and explore
the lush landscapes that make it a unique destination in Baguio.

Tam-Awan Village

Tam-awan Village in Baguio, Philippines, is an artist's village and cultural center that showcases the heritage and traditions of indigenous Cordilleran communities. It features reconstructed traditional huts, art galleries, and workshops where visitors
can learn about the region's indigenous culture, art, and crafts.
The village offers a glimpse into the rich cultural diversity of the Cordillera region and provides a unique cultural and artistic experience for visitors.
